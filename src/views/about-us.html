Who We Are

A dedicated team with a mission to deliver quality, embodying integrity and pushing the boundaries of what's possible.

Established in 1998, Accord Security Pty Ltd is one of WA’s longest established security providers. We are a private, wholly-WA owned company supplying services and personnel to a wide range of businesses from national retail chains and manufacturers to warehouses, government departments and various other industries. With a highly experienced professional management team and strong infrastructure we are capable of providing a comprehensive range of security services.

Accord Security Pty Ltd began with one single mobile patrol contract and within five short years, we became an entity with over 120 security personnel, Providing coverage in excess of over 4,000 man hours per week.

Today we hold many prestigious contracts for a number of well-known national and international corporations and government departments.

To provide an idea of the level at which Accord Security operates, here is a list of some of our various past and present clients clients:

· CB Richard Ellis
· City of Kwinana
· City of Mandurah
· City of Rockingham (City Of Swan)
· De Francesch Building Pty Ltd
· Frederick Irwin Anglican School
· Harvey Norman (· Lease Equity · Town of Claremont · Venues West)

The success and strength of any company lies in its capability to acquire dedicated professionals who work hand in hand. Accord Security Pty Ltd does this by allowing all levels of workers, from management through to each standalone security officer, a climate of responsibility, appreciation and respect. This enables every individual to develop to their full potential, whilst working as a team to accomplish business objectives and meet client expectations.

It is the aim of Accord Security Pty Ltd to provide a first class service, which exceeds the standards and expectations of our clients in all areas of manpower-based security.

Accord Security is a fully-licensed private limited company – Security Licence number 0020 – and is a proud member of the Australian Security Industry Association Ltd (ASIAL).
